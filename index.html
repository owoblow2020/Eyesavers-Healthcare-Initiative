CHANGING LIVES

Mrs. Cecelia's astonishment knew no bounds as she held the written text before her, her fingers tracing the words with newfound clarity. For years, she had resigned herself to a world blurred by the darkness of impaired vision. But on this extraordinary day, the Eyesavers Healthcare Initiative brought forth a beacon of hope, generously providing free reading glasses to the members of the Gwazunu community.
