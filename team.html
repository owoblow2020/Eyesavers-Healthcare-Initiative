Dr. Wisdom Ebeogu

Founder & Chairman, BoT.

Dr. Wisdom Ebeogu, an Optometrist with a Master's Degree in Public Health, brings over a decade of healthcare experience to her role as the visionary founder of Eyesavers Healthcare Initiative. Her journey began during her service term as a corps member in Niger State, Northern Nigeria, where she dedicated a full year to conducting numerous outreaches aimed at restoring sight and promoting healthy living within underserved communities.

Dr. Anthony Emmanuel Agada

Board of Trustees Member

Dr. Anthony Emmanuel Agada is passionate about youth empowerment and acknowledging their potential to impact the future. He is a dedicated public servant and the CEO of Pinnacle Geosciences Ltd. in Jos. He has been guided by strong integrity and a commitment to ethical standards in his personal and professional life.

OLUWAFUNKE ADEOYE Esq.

Board of Trustees Member

Oluwafunke Adeoye is an Award-winning Attorney and Social Entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in human rights and criminal justice. She holds a law degree from the University of Benin, Nigeria and is a Commonwealth Scholar at the University of Oxford. Since 2019, Funke has been an expert respondent for the World Justice Project Rule of Law Index. She is a 2019 Cornell University Makwayane Fellow, an Acumen West Africa Fellow and a Mandela Washington Fellow.
