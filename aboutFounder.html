Dr. Wisdom Ebeogu, a passionate Optometrist

Dr. Wisdom Ebeogu, an Optometrist with a Master's Degree in Public Health, brings over a decade of healthcare experience to her role as the visionary founder of Eyesavers Healthcare Initiative. Her journey began during her service term as a corps member in Niger State, Northern Nigeria, where she dedicated a full year to conducting numerous outreaches aimed at restoring sight and promoting healthy living within underserved communities.

  • A Path-finder.
  • An Innovator
  • A Motivator

Collaborating closely with community leaders and garnering strong local support, Dr. Ebeogu overcame challenges to establish Eyesavers Healthcare Initiative as a leading force in promoting eye health and providing essential healthcare services. Her unwavering commitment has resulted in the outreach and transformation of numerous lives, ensuring that no one is left behind due to vision impairment or lack of access to healthcare.
